What is Object Detection?

Object detection involves identifying and labeling specific objects in a video. This is commonly used in security systems, autonomous driving, and video analytics to track movements or detect abnormal behavior.

Applications of Object Detection

  • Automated surveillance systems
  • Traffic monitoring and vehicle detection
  • People tracking and counting in large areas
